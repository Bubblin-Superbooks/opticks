By the discovered Properties of Light to explain the Colours of the Rain-bow.

![Fig. 14.](images/fig14-2.jpg Fig. 14.)

This Bow never appears, but where it rains in the Sun-shine, and may be made artificially by spouting up Water which may break aloft, and scatter into Drops, and fall down like Rain. For the Sun shining upon these Drops certainly causes the Bow to appear to a Spectator standing in a due Position to the Rain and Sun.
