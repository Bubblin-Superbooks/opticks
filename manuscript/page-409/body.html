Prop. Iv.

The Parts of Bodies and their Interstices must not be less than of some definite bigness, to render them opake and colour'd.

For the opakest Bodies, if their parts be subtilly divided, (as Metals, by being dissolved in acid Menstruums, &c. become perfectly transparent.
