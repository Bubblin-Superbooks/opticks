Prop. Vi.

The parts of Bodies on which their Colours depend, are denser than the Medium which pervades their Interstices.

This will appear by considering, that the Colour of a Body depends not only on the Rays which are incident perpendicularly on its parts, but on those also which are incident at all other Angles.
